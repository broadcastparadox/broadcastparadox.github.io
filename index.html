The Broadcast Paradox

Welcome to The Broadcast Paradox, a thrilling journey into the unknown where you will unravel the mysteries of an alien signal!

An enigmatic transmission has been detected, revealing fragments of an extraterrestrial message. It is up to you to decipher the anomalies, recover the audio, and uncover the alien's assessment of Earth.

Game Features:

  • Scan and monitor radio signals to identify hidden anomalies.
  • Decrypt audio fragments containing steganographic images of Earth's landmarks.
  • Work together or compete with others to recover the full alien message.
  • Upgrade your tools to enhance signal detection and decryption speed.

Are you ready to piece together the alien transmission and solve the ultimate mystery?
